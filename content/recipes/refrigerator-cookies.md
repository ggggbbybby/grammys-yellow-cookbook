* ½ cup butter
* ⅔ cup brown sugar
* ⅓ cup white sugar
* 1 egg, beaten
* 1½ cup flour
* ⅓ tsp soda
* ¼ tsp salt
* 1 tsp vanilla

1. Mix.
2. Shape in roll.
3. Wrap in wax paper and store in refrigerator.
4. Slice off and bake on buttered cookie sheet for 8 minutes at 400.

_good with black walnuts_
