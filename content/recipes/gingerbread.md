* 1 cup molasses
* 1/3 cup butter
* 1 1/2 tsp soda
* 1/2 cup sour milk
* 1 egg, beaten
* 2 cups flour
* 2 tsp ginger
* 1/2 tsp salt

1. Add butter to molasses and heat until butter melts. Add soda and beat hard.
2. Add milk, egg, flour, ginger, and salt. Beat well.
4. Pour into a layer cake pan. Bake at 325 for 25-35 minutes.
