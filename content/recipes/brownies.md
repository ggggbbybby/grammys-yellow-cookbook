* 1 cup butter, melted
* 2 cups sugar
* 4 heaping tbsp cocoa
* 1 1/2 cups flour
* 4 eggs
* 2 tsp vanilla
* salt to taste
* 2 cups pecans, chopped

1. Mix all ingredients.
2. Bake at 325 for 25 minutes in a 15x10" pan. 

#### Icing
* 4 tbsp butter
* 3/4 box powdered sugar
* 1 egg
* 2 tbsp rum
* 2 tbsp cocoa

Mix, and spread over brownies while still hot.
