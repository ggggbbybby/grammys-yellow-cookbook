Refrigerator Cookies

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/3 tsp soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  1. Mix.
  2. Shape in roll.
  3. Wrap in wax paper and store in refrigerator.
  4. Slice off and bake on buttered cookie sheet for 8 minutes at 400.

good with black walnuts
